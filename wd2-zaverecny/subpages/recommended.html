Recommended

Metal has something for nearly everyone, here is my starter recommendations for nonmetalheads

Rap

- for rap fans there are bands like slipknot that are known for mixing rap and metal together

- here´s an example of very known band called slipknot with song "spit it out"

Pop

- there´s so much stuff for pop fans, even though some metalheads don´t like it

- a band called Tesseract is an example of this, image on right is cover of their newest album

- left example is a band called tesseract and their "poplike" song "hexes", on the right is a band jinjer with song "pisces"

Classical

- Tesseract is also band that has big influences from classical music, too

- but there are bands like System Of A Down that combines metal with Armenian traditional music or Tool that have some very complex and long songs

- first one is tool´s song "Schizm" and the next is a System Of A down song called "Holy Mountains"

Funk/Jazz

- it´s not as evident as in other genres, if you listen to bass in band called Mudvayne, you can clearly hear a really funky basslines, their album cover on the right leads you to their song "death blooms"

- also there is a band called Animals As Leaders that has a big influence in jazz, here´s an example of them with song called "woven web"
