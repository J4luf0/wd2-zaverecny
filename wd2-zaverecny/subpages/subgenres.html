Subgenres

as already said, metal has a really lot subgenres, lets look at the most known of them

1. Heavy Metal

- started it all, it´s the basis of all other subgenres

- it begin to be popular at the end of 60´s in the USA and Great Britain

- sometime "heavy metal" is used to name whole metal genre but we keep it

to use only for this metal subgenre

- bands typical fo this subgenre are mentioned in the introduction

2. Progressive metal

- combines agressive sound and heavily disorted guitars with very soft melodic parts

- uses a lots of odd time signatures and complex melodies, also a lot experimentation

- bands like:  Tesseract  and  Dream Theater

3. Thrash Metal

- this metal subgenre is something like a lot faster brother of heavy metal

- started in the 80´s and has influences from hardcore punk

- most known bands are:   Metallica   and  Slayer
